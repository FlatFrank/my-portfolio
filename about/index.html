Long bio

Paco is a product designer who focuses on turning complex systems into clear, usable experiences. Over the past several years, Paco has worked across early-stage startups and larger orgs, partnering closely with product and engineering to ship features end-to-end. Typical work includes discovery, UX flows, prototyping, design systems, and writing that helps teams align.

Paco cares about craft, but prioritizes outcomes: clearer mental models, fewer edge-case failures, and faster decision-making. When there’s ambiguity, Paco brings structure through lightweight frameworks, clear documentation, and iterative delivery.
